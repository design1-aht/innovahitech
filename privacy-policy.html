Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Innova Hi-Tech handles personal information submitted through this website.

Scope

This policy covers information collected through forms and contact options provided on the Innova Hi-Tech website. It does not extend to third-party websites or services that may be linked from this site.

Information Collected

When you use the contact form or send an email to Innova Hi-Tech, you may provide information such as your name, email address, phone number, organisation details, and the content of your enquiry.

Use of Information

Information you submit is used to respond to your enquiry, coordinate with you or your organisation, and, where relevant, maintain basic records for support and compliance purposes. Information is not sold or rented to third parties.

Disclosure

Information may be shared with authorised internal teams or trusted service providers only where necessary to respond to your enquiry or to comply with legal, regulatory, or security obligations. Any such sharing is limited to the purpose for which the information was originally provided.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to reduce the risk of unauthorised access, misuse, or loss of information submitted via this website. No method of transmission or storage is completely risk-free, and users are encouraged not to include highly sensitive personal data in website forms.

Retention

Contact information and related correspondence may be retained for as long as required to fulfil the purpose of the communication, support customer interactions, or meet legal and regulatory requirements.

Your Choices

If you wish to update or request deletion of information you have previously submitted, you can contact Innova Hi-Tech using the contact details published on this site. Such requests will be handled in line with applicable law and internal retention obligations.
