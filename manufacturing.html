Manufacturing & Processes

Industrial processes aligned around accuracy, repeatability, and controlled assembly.

CNC Machining

Key parts such as receivers, mounts, and precision brackets are produced using CNC machining centers. This helps maintain dimensional accuracy, alignment, and surface finish where it matters most.

Barrel Rifling

Barrel rifling processes are controlled to provide stable pellet rotation and predictable trajectories. This contributes directly to grouping performance and shooter confidence at the range.

Assembly & Quality Control

Each air gun passes through staged assembly, functional inspection, and quality checks. Leak tests, trigger function checks, and visual inspections are part of the release process.

Process Orientation

Processes are documented and repeatable, so that each batch of air guns is built to the same expectations. Incoming materials, machining parameters, and assembly steps are aligned for consistency.

Continuous Improvement

Feedback from dealers, shooting clubs, and trainers is used to refine designs and manufacturing methods. This approach helps invoahitech improve performance and usability over time.
