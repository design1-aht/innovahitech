About invoahitech

Dedicated to building reliable, accurate air guns for the Indian shooting community.

Company Overview

invoahitech is an air gun manufacturing company based in the industrial area of Malot, Teh. Indora, Distt. Kangra, Himachal Pradesh, India. The company focuses on engineered products that combine precision machining, controlled assembly, and rigorous quality checks.

Our product range includes air rifles, air pistols, and supporting accessories designed for shooting clubs, training academies, and responsible individual shooters.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To design and manufacture dependable air guns that help shooters practice with confidence, repeatability, and safety.

Vision: To be a trusted Indian brand for air guns, recognized for consistent performance, customer support, and a responsible approach to the shooting sports ecosystem.

Made in India Manufacturing

All key manufacturing activities are carried out in India. This allows invoahitech to control specifications, material selection, and process flow from raw material intake to final assembly. Being located in Himachal Pradesh also positions the company close to industrial suppliers and skilled technical talent.

Quality and Safety Focus

Each air gun passes through multiple checkpoints covering component inspection, sub-assembly verification, leak checks, and functional testing. Designs are reviewed with a focus on safety, reliability, and ease of maintenance, so that users can concentrate on training and performance.
